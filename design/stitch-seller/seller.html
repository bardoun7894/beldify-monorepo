Four Generations of Fassi Brocade

Nestled in the heart of the ancient medina, Atelier Fassi has been weaving stories into fabric since 1958. Our heritage is rooted in the meticulous art of Fassi brocade, a technique passed down through four generations. Each caftan and djellaba is a testament to the patience and precision of our master artisans, who spend weeks hand-stitching intricate motifs that celebrate Moroccan elegance.

We blend ancestral wisdom with contemporary refinement, sourcing only the finest silks and natural fibers. Our workshop remains a sanctuary of traditional craftsmanship in an ever-accelerating world, dedicated to preserving the soulful artistry of Fez for a discerning global clientele.
