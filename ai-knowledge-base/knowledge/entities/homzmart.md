# Homzmart

## What it is
Homzmart (هومزمارت) is an Egyptian e-commerce marketplace for furniture and home goods at homzmart.com, Arabic-first/RTL with an English toggle, EGP pricing, and heavy consumer-financing promotion (ValU 0% installments). Beldify used it as the primary competitive reference for home-page merchandising structure in June 2026.

## Key facts
- Pure white canvas; Roboto; sections separated by whitespace and full-width campaign banners rather than background color changes.
- Three-layer sticky header: red promo marquee (rotating offers + codes), navy header with large centered search and deliver-to selector, white category nav where every category has a product photo thumbnail (photo tiles pinned under the header on mobile).
- Hero is a campaign banner carousel with dots/arrows and a single "اشتري الآن" chip — merchandising space, not a brand statement.
- Product cards behave as mini-PDPs: lifestyle room-scene photos, availability badge, rating + count, starts-from pricing with strikethrough + discount %, variant chips (e.g. "4 المقاسات، 2 نوع").
- Shop-by-room mental model ("تسوق حسب الغرفة") and department zones (kids) acting as mini store landings inside the home page.
