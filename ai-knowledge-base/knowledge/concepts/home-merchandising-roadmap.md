# Home Merchandising Roadmap

The Beldify home page adopts marketplace merchandising structure from the Homzmart analysis in phases, explicitly preserving brand identity: the neutral near-white canvas with indigo brand bands and amber accents stays, and OpenSouk remains the only indigo full-bleed band, made more distinct rather than flattened.

Phase 0 is content, not code: real product, category, and hero photography — the analysis found imagery, not layout, was the dominant gap (dead Unsplash seeds rendered cards as blank frames). Phase 1 is the merchandising layer: a promo marquee bar above the header (indigo background, amber text, rotating offers/codes), sticky mobile category photo tiles, and upgrading product cards toward mini-PDPs (ratings everywhere, "يبدأ من" starts-from pricing for multi-variant and tailoring items, variant chips, discount strikethrough, availability badges). Phase 2 adds campaign surfaces: the hero carousel (shipped as [[concepts/hero-admin-switch]]) and admin-managed mid-page banners between rails. Phase 3 changes mental models: a "تسوق حسب المناسبة" occasion grid (wedding, Eid, summer, daily, gifts — Beldify's analog of Homzmart's shop-by-room) and department zones such as a wedding corner and a jewelry zone.

For OpenSouk, the roadmap calls for trading-floor energy in the request cards — pulsing live offer counts (proposal counts are public under the blind-bidding model), amber budget chips, freshness indicators, a prominent "قدّم عرضك" CTA, and optionally an auto-scrolling ticker of the latest requests.
