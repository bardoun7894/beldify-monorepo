## Root cause of the layout issue

When I used **logical properties** (`margin-inline-*`) + `unset` fallbacks for the physical ones in my override, the logical-physical cascade resolution under `!important` got muddled ŌĆö the browser was computing 0 for both margins despite my `!important` declarations.
